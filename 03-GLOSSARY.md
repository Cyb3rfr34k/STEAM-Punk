# Glossary (Night Shift Edition)

- **SIEM**: Security Information and Event Management; the system that collects and correlates logs across the enterprise. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it’s the only witness who doesn’t sleep.
- **EDR**: Endpoint Detection and Response; monitoring and response tools on devices. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it tells you what the device did, not what the user says.
- **IOC**: Indicator of Compromise (hash, IP, domain, file path). *Why it matters in the night shift:* IOCs are the breadcrumbs you can hand to the morning crew.
- **TTP**: Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures; how adversaries operate. *Why it matters in the night shift:* patterns are the only way to see the invisible.
- **KQL**: Kusto Query Language for Microsoft Sentinel queries. *Why it matters in the night shift:* you don’t hunt without a shovel.
- **Microsoft Sentinel**: Cloud-native SIEM and SOAR platform. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it decides whether you panic or breathe.
- **MDE (Microsoft Defender for Endpoint)**: Endpoint detection platform. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it provides the device timeline that ends arguments.
- **Entra ID (Azure AD)**: Identity and access management for users and apps. *Why it matters in the night shift:* identities are the doorway, and doors are where you count bodies.
- **Exchange Online**: Cloud email platform. *Why it matters in the night shift:* email is the delivery truck for half of the trouble.
- **Risky Sign-In**: Entra ID classification indicating anomalous or risky login behavior. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it’s a warning label, not a verdict.
- **Impossible Travel**: Logins from distant locations in implausible time. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it teaches you about VPNs, time zones, and lies.
- **Device Isolation**: Cutting a device off the network via EDR. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it’s surgery without anesthesia.
- **Process Tree**: Parent/child process relationships on a device. *Why it matters in the night shift:* the tree shows you who whispered to whom.
- **Mailbox Rule**: Automated actions on incoming mail. *Why it matters in the night shift:* rules can silently hide the crime.
- **Token Replay**: Reusing authentication tokens to access accounts. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it looks like a normal login until it doesn’t.
- **Containment**: Steps to limit attacker movement. *Why it matters in the night shift:* it’s the only part that feels like control.
